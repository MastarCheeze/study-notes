# Indices

## Properties

$$
\begin{aligned}
  a^m \cdot a^n &= a^{m+n} \\
  \frac{a^m}{a^n} &= a^{m-n} \\
  (a^m)^n &= a^{mn} \\
  \sqrt[n]{a^m} &= a^\frac{m}{n} \\
  a^{-m} &= \frac{1}{a^m}
\end{aligned}
$$

## Example Questions

1. Same base
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
     3^x &= 27 \\
     3^x &= 3^3 \\
     x &= 3
   \end{aligned}
   $$

2. Different base
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
     3^x &= 2 \\
     x &= \log_32
   \end{aligned}
   $$

3. Single term (linear)
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
     5^{x+1}-5^x &= 8 \\
     5(5^x)-5^x &= 8 \\
     4(5^x) &= 8 \\
     5^x &= 2 \\
     x &= \log_52
   \end{aligned}
   $$

4. Single term (quadratic)
   $$
   \begin{aligned}
     9^x-3^{x+1} &= 4 \\
     (3^{x})^2-3(3^x) -4 &= 0 \\
     (3^x-4)(3^x+1) &= 0 \\
     \text{(1)} \quad 3^x &= 4 \\
     x &= \log_34 \\
     \text{(2)} \quad 3^x &= -1 \quad \text{(no solution)} \\
   \end{aligned}
   $$
