# Linear Equations

## Graphs

### Equation of line

$$
\begin{aligned}
  y &= mx + c \\
  y - y_1 &= m(x - x_1)
\end{aligned}
$$

### Gradient of line

$$m = \frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1} = \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}$$

## Geometric

### Length of line

$$l = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2}$$

### Midpoint of two points

$$M\left(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1+y_2}{2}\right)$$

### Gradient of perpendicular line

$$
\begin{aligned}
  m_1 \cdot m_2 &= -1 \\
  m_1 &= -\frac{1}{m_2}
\end{aligned}
$$
