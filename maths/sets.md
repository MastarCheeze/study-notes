# Sets

## Notation

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \xi &= \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\} \enspace \scriptsize\text{(universal set)} \\
  A &= \{1, 2, 3\} \\
  B &= \{3, 4, 5\} \\
  C &= \{\} = \varnothing \enspace \scriptsize\text{(empty set)}
\end{aligned}
$$

Set builder notation

$$
D = \{x : x \text{ is an integer, } 0 < x < 20\}
$$

### Member of

$$
\begin{aligned}
  1 &\in A \\
  4 &\notin A
\end{aligned}
$$

### Subset

Proper subset $\subset$ - both sets cannot be equal \
Improper subset $\subseteq$ - both sets can be equal

$$
\begin{aligned}
  \{1, 2\} &\subset A \\
  \{1, 2, 3\} &\subseteq A \\
  \{1, 2\} &\not\subset B \\
  \{1, 2, 3\} &\not\subseteq B \\
\end{aligned}
$$

## Operators

### Complement

$$
A' = \{4, 5\}
$$

### Union

$$
A \cup B = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}
$$

### Intersection

$$
A \cap B = \{3\}
$$

### Number of elements

$$
\mathrm{n}(A) = 3
$$
