# Power

## Force

> $$
\begin{aligned}
  P &= \frac{E}{t} \\
  \mathrm{power} &= \mathrm{\frac{energy~transferred}{time~taken}}
\end{aligned}
> $$
> Unit: Watt (W) \
> Energy transferred per unit time

## Electricity

> $$
\begin{aligned}
  P &= VI \\
  \mathrm{power} &= \mathrm{voltage \cdot current}
\end{aligned}
> $$
>
> Unit: Watt (W)

Derived from $P=VI$:
> $$
\begin{aligned}
  E &= VIt \\
  \mathrm{energy} &= \mathrm{voltage \cdot current \cdot time}
\end{aligned}
> $$
