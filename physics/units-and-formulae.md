## Formulae

### Heat
$$
\begin{aligned}
  K &= C + 273
\end{aligned}
$$

### Electricity

$$
\begin{aligned}
  I &= \frac{Q}{t} \\
  V &= \frac{E}{Q} \\
  V &= IR \\
  P &= \frac{E}{t} \\
  P &= VI \\
  E &= VIt
\end{aligned}
$$
