# Fission

> **Splitting** one large nucleus into two smaller nuclei \
> Emits neutrons

$$\ce{^235_92U + ^1_0n -> ^144_56Ba + ^90_36Kr + 2^1_0n}$$

# Fusion

> **Combining** two small nuclei into one large nucleus \
> Emits neutrons \
> Requires high temperature

$$\ce{^2_1H + ^3_1H -> ^4_2He + ^1_0n}$$
